# BeLLMark Licensing FAQ

Answers to common questions about BeLLMark licenses, permitted use, and purchasing.

---

## 1. What is a "legal entity" for licensing?

A legal entity is an organization that can enter into contracts and make payments under a single registered name. One license covers one legal entity.

- **Subsidiaries are separate entities.** A parent company license does not extend to subsidiaries unless they share the same registration number.
- **Sole proprietor / single-member LLC**: Typically counts as one entity. One license covers your business.
- **Parent with subsidiaries**: Each subsidiary is a separate entity and requires its own license.

The rule of thumb: one company registration number = one entity = one license.

---

## 2. How do we determine if our use is "commercial"?

Apply the conservative rule: **if BeLLMark is used by or for a for-profit entity, or used to support revenue-generating work, the use is commercial.**

This includes:

- Internal use at any for-profit company, regardless of whether BeLLMark itself is sold
- Consulting or services work where the client pays you (even if BeLLMark is not line-itemed)
- Research at a for-profit company, including R&D departments

If you are genuinely unsure, email support@bellmark.ai. For non-commercial exemptions, see Q11.

---

## 3. I'm a freelance consultant. Do my clients need licenses?

No. One **€799 Commercial License** covers your use of BeLLMark for all client engagements. You run BeLLMark; your clients receive outputs (reports, exports). Outputs are owned by you and may be delivered to clients without restriction.

Your clients do not need to purchase licenses unless they are running BeLLMark themselves.

---

## 4. Are the outputs (reports/exports) restricted?

No. Benchmark results, reports, and all export formats (HTML, PDF, PPTX, CSV, JSON) are owned by the license buyer. You may share, publish, or deliver them to clients without restriction.

---

## 5. Is there a license key or activation?

No. BeLLMark uses receipt-based licensing. There are no license keys, no activation servers, and no phone-home telemetry. Your receipt or invoice is your proof of license. Keep it on file.

---

## 6. What support is included?

All licenses include best-effort async email support covering:

- Installation and environment setup
- Provider configuration (API keys, local models)
- Configuration questions
- Export troubleshooting

Support is provided on a best-effort basis and does not constitute a Service Level Agreement (SLA). Response times are typically within 5 business days.

---

## 7. How are updates delivered?

Updates are delivered via the project repository. Pull the latest tagged release to update your installation. We recommend pinning to a specific tagged release for internal change control, and reviewing the changelog before upgrading in production environments.

---

## 8. What is NOT permitted?

The following uses are not permitted under the noncommercial license:

- **Commercial use**: You may not use BeLLMark for commercial purposes without a commercial license
- **Hosted service (SaaS)**: You may not offer BeLLMark as a hosted service to third parties, even free of charge
- **Sublicensing or reselling**: You may not sell or sublicense access to the software

If you need to discuss a use case not covered by the standard license, contact support@bellmark.ai.

---

## 9. Do you sign security questionnaires, DPAs, BAAs?

v1 policy: We do not sign custom contracts, security questionnaires, Data Processing Agreements (DPAs), or Business Associate Agreements (BAAs).

Security review should be satisfied by:

1. **Published documentation** covering architecture, data flow, and security posture
2. **The self-hosted model**: BeLLMark runs entirely on your infrastructure. No data is sent to BeLLMark servers. There is zero telemetry.

If your procurement process requires custom agreements, BeLLMark v1 is not the right fit at this time.

**Self-serve**: Card checkout is available at the product page. Your receipt is your license.

**Invoice / Purchase Order**: Email support@bellmark.ai with the following:

- Legal entity name
- Billing address
- Buyer contact name and email
- Quantity (number of entities)
- PO number (if applicable)

Invoice payment terms are Net 30 unless otherwise agreed.

## 11. Non-profit / academic use?

BeLLMark is **free** for the following organizations:

- Registered charitable organizations (501(c)(3) or equivalent)
- Educational institutions (universities, colleges, schools)
- Public research organizations (government-funded research labs)
- Government institutions

To confirm your eligibility, email support@bellmark.ai with your organization name and documentation of your status. No purchase is required for qualifying organizations.

---

## 12. Startup (pre-revenue)?

Startups are generally commercial entities and require a paid license, even if pre-revenue. The basis for the license requirement is the for-profit nature of the entity, not whether it has generated revenue yet.

If your startup qualifies for a different exception (e.g., university spinout still under an academic umbrella), contact support@bellmark.ai.

---

## 13. Internal innovation lab?

If your innovation lab or internal team is part of the same legal entity as the purchasing organization, one license covers it. The rule: **one license per company registration number**, regardless of internal team structure, branding, or cost center.

If the lab is a separately incorporated entity (e.g., a corporate venture arm with its own registration), it requires a separate license.

---

## 14. Transition from non-commercial to commercial use?

BeLLMark is licensed under PolyForm Noncommercial for non-commercial use. The PolyForm Noncommercial license includes a **first-violation cure clause**: if you begin commercial use without a paid license, you have **32 days from the date of notification** to come into compliance by purchasing a Commercial License.

We recommend purchasing before beginning commercial use rather than relying on the cure period.
